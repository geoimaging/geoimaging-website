Welcome to the
GeoImaging Research Group
at Virginia Tech

We work on developing non-invasive images of the earth's subsurface for engineering applications.
Our work is situated at the intersection of geotechnical engineering and near-surface geophysics and
uses tools from applied mathematics, statistics and data science, and scientific computing.
