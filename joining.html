Currently Recruiting for:
Graduate Student Researcher

Posted: 6 October 2024 | Status: Closed Will Open 15 October 2024

Position Description

This posting is for a funded PhD position under the supervision of Professor Joseph P. Vantassel in the geotechnical engineering program at Virginia Tech. The position start date is October 15 2024. The position will involve applying state-of-the-art subsurface imaging techniques to near-surface geotechnical site characterization. The position will involve field-based data acquisition, data processing and interpretation, and publication of results. The position will require software development and use of high performance computing. The position may involve substantial utilization of machine & deep learning. The ideal candidate will have strong communication skills (written and oral) and a proven ability to deliver on projects.

Required Qualifications

  • MS degree in geotechnical engineering or related field.
  • Ability to pursue PhD in civil engineering (geotechnical focus) at Virginia Tech in the fall semester of 2024.
  • Prior experience in geotechnical engineering, near-surface geophysics, or other related field.
  • Prior programming experience (any language).
  • Ability to perform field-based research.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Prior experience with software development (Python or C++ preferred).
  • Knowledge of high performance computing concepts.
  • Familiarity with inverse problems.

Timeline

Review of candidates will begin immediately and end January 1st.
